AS ABOVE, SO BELOW

PROJECT

As above, So below

CATEGORY

Exhibition, Visual Identity

YEAR

2023

INFO

The key visual of KABK photography department exhibition extracts two key words: connection and network. I use blue circular stickers and black sticky notes to form a combination. This combination can be composed and developed at in different ways. The negative space between them establishes the connection line between each blue circular sticker. Once the entire visual system is built, it can be applied to anything, posters, flyers, social media, exhibition setting, etc. In order to adapt to the party activities at night, a purple version is also added.‍

简介

海牙皇家艺术学院摄影系展览的主视觉，提取了联结和网络两个关键词。我利用蓝色圆形贴纸和黑色便签纸构成一个组合，这个组合可以随意地被拼接变化，它们中间的负空间建立起了每个蓝色圆形贴纸之间的联结。搭建起整个视觉系统后，可以将这个组合应用到任何的媒介上，海报，手册，社交媒体，展览布置等。为了适配夜晚的派对活动，也额外地增加了紫色版本。
