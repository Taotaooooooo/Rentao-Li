Introduction

Rentao Li is a graphic designer and visual artist who grew up in China. He holds a bachelor's degree from the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague and is currently pursuing a master's degree in Visual Communication at Aalto University in Finland. As a multidisciplinary designer, he specializes in poster design, motion graphics, web development, 3D creation, and video production. Passionate about learning, conceptualizing, and crafting beautiful visual works, he strives to convey unique aesthetics and profound ideas through his designs.
